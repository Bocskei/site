Hi, I'm

Dániel Bőcskei

I am a software developer and hobby game developer from Hungary.

I finished Computer Science BsC at Eötvös Loránd University. I have been learning programming since I was 14 years old, but I've been interested in making games ever since I was able to play them. What makes me passionate about games is the amount of creativity and vision of a person or a team can create life changing experiences for others.
My professional experience in the gaming industry started as a Test Associate for AAA popular MMOs.
I participated in several gamejams and had some hobby projects over the years. Here are only a few projects of mine or that I've had the opportunity of contributing to.

Pinball

Unreal Engine 5 C++ Detailed Documentation

For my Bachelor's Thesis, I developed a physics-based game in Unreal Engine 5 over six months, this was my first bigger project in UE5.
Featuring a dynamic point scoring system, game over mechanics, multipliers, and wormholes, the game offers multiple ways to bounce actors using built-in physics and custom components.

Platform: PC
Duration: 6 months
Tools Used: C++, Blueprints, Unreal Insights

Rhythm Arcade

Godot GDScript C# Small Team Project

A Rhythm game project made with a few of my friends for a public event of a few hundred People.
My main role was the point scoring system and game feel of hitting notes while playing.

Platform Raspberry Pi
Duration: 2 months
Tools Used: GDScript, C
